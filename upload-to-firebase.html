📋 First time? Create Firestore database:

  1. Open Firebase Console → Firestore Database
  2. Click "Create database"
  3. Select "Start in test mode" → click Next
  4. Choose location "asia-south1" → click Done
  5. Wait 30 seconds for it to be ready
  6. Come back here and click the upload button above ☝️
